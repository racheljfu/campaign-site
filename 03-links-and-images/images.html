Images

This page covers common image formats but you may also be looking for links and useful extras.

JPGs

JPG images are good for photos with lots of gradients.

GIFs

GIFs are good for animations, but are limited in terms of color palette and it can be hard to get high levels of detail.

PNGs

PNGs are good for anything that's not photographs or animations, like icons or diagrams. They deal with opacity and color just fine.

SVGs

SVGs are vector-based graphics, so it's a good tool for responsive design. They're good for the same uses as PNGs, for them to display consistently across browsers, you need to convert any text fields to outlines using your image editor.
