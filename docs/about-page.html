Rachel Wonder is a central Pennsylvania-based artist and second-year undergraduate at the University of Pittsburgh. She is pursuing a Public and Professional Writing degree with minors in English Literature and Chinese.

Since 2018, Rachel has worked seasonally as a volunteer instructor at the educational arts studio Work of HeART. As of 2019, she currently serves as a copy editor at The Pitt News and as an ESL tutor with FORGE at Pitt.
